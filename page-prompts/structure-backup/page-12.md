## CHARACTER DESCRIPTIONS

### COREY (protagonist)
- **Physical**: Completely BALD head, staring at nothing or with head in hands
- **Clothing**: Navy blue apron untied and hanging loose
- **Posture**: Sitting alone in customer chair (not behind counter), defeated
- **Expression**: Profound sadness, feeling lost and empty
- **Mood**: At his lowest emotional point, convinced his dream has failed

- **Appearance**: Shown from behind or in silhouette

- **Mood**: Sympathetic but needing to move on

- **Dining Room**: Completely EMPTY with chairs pushed in, no customers

- **Equipment**: Espresso machine dark and cold, no steam or warmth

- **Lighting**: Pale, sad lighting instead of previous warm glow

### WALK-IN FREEZER (SYMBOLIC OF FAILURE)
- **Contents**: PERSONIFIED sad food - wilting vegetables with droopy eyes
- **Details**: Meat packages with frowns, ice cream cartons crying melted tears
- **Produce**: Items going bad and looking apologetic
- **Door**: Hanging open, representing defeat

### SYMBOLIC ELEMENTS
- **Three Years**: Three symbolic objects representing his time - three coffee cups on table, three aprons on hooks, or three calendars
- **Soul**: The Store's "soul" feels departed, no longer a living community hub
