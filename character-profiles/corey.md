# Character Profile: COREY
*The Protagonist - The Chef with Dreams*

## PHYSICAL CHARACTERISTICS

### Head & Hair
- **COMPLETELY BALD** (no hair whatsoever - this is his most distinctive feature)
- **Warm, friendly face** with expressive features
- **Clean-shaven face** (no beard or stubble)
- **Medium build, fit physique** (not skinny, not heavy - healthy chef build)

### Facial Features
- **BIG GENUINE SMILE** - his defining characteristic, warm and infectious
- **Kind, expressive eyes** that show his emotions clearly
- **Approachable, friendly demeanor** - the kind of person people naturally trust
- **Age appearance**: 30s-40s adult

## CLOTHING & COSTUMES

### Regular Work Attire
- **Navy blue apron** (his signature look when working)
- **Casual clothes underneath**: Long-sleeve gray or black shirt
- **Professional but approachable** kitchen appearance

### Fantasy Sequence Costumes
Transforms into various outfits for career fantasies:
- **Astronaut**: Retro-futuristic spacesuit with extra tubes and buttons
- **Punk Rocker**: Black leather jacket with studs, spiked mohawk WIG (still bald underneath)
- **Sea Captain**: Naval coat with brass buttons, captain's hat with gold braid
- **Politician**: Ill-fitting suit (more comfortable in apron)
- **Lawyer**: Sharp professional suit with "ESQUIRE" business card
- **And many more...**

## PERSONALITY & CHARACTERISTICS

### Core Traits
- **Hardworking and determined** - commits fully to his dreams
- **Resilient** - faces setbacks but doesn't give up until the very end
- **Family-oriented** - his love for his family is his greatest strength
- **Optimistic** - naturally sees possibilities and potential
- **Genuine** - authentic and real, not putting on airs

### Emotional Journey
- **Book 1**: Hopeful → Successful → Increasingly worried → Defeated
- **Books 2-4**: Surprised by rescue → Inspired by possibilities → Overwhelmed by choices
- **Book 5**: Transformed → Confident → Ready for anything

### Relationships
- **Emily**: His rock, his partner, his source of strength
- **The Super Three**: His beloved children who see infinite potential in him
- **Community**: Naturally builds connections, people are drawn to his warmth

## DR. SEUSS ART STYLE NOTES

### Visual Characteristics
- **Bald head should be prominent** in all illustrations
- **Exaggerate his warm smile** when happy
- **Show emotion clearly** through facial expressions
- **Medium proportions** - not too tall or short in Seussian style
- **When sad**: Slumped shoulders, head hanging
- **When happy**: Upright posture, arms spread wide, beaming smile

### Color Palette
- **Apron**: Navy blue (his signature color)
- **Skin tone**: Natural, warm tones
- **Fantasy costumes**: Bold, saturated Seussian colors
- **Emotional states**: 
  - Happy scenes: Warm golds, oranges, bright colors
  - Sad scenes: Cool blues, grays, muted tones

## STORY ROLE
Corey represents the everyman who faces setbacks but discovers that with family support, every ending can be a new beginning. His baldness makes him instantly recognizable, while his warm smile shows his genuine heart. He's the chef who learns that the most important ingredient in any recipe for success is love and family support.
