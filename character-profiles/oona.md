# Character Profile: OONA
*The Joyful Twin - The Bright Spirit*

## PHYSICAL CHARACTERISTICS

### Hair & Head
- **Long light brown/honey blonde hair** - her most distinctive feature
- **Hair is always in motion** - bouncing, flying, flowing as she moves
- **Beautiful honey-colored tones** that catch light
- **Length reaches mid-back or longer**

### Facial Features & Build
- **Age**: 11 years old
- **Twin to Remi** - same height and build as her brother
- **BRIGHT, joyful expression** - perpetually happy and energetic
- **Often jumping or in motion** - she's literally bouncing with joy
- **Radiant energy** - lights up any scene she's in

## CLOTHING & ACCESSORIES

### Signature Outfit
- **BLUE T-SHIRT** with **SUPER3 LOGO** prominently displayed
- **Logo Details**: Red diamond shield with large yellow "3" in center, plus small letters "Z", "O", "R" in corners (for Zephyr, Oona, Remi) - see reference image: images/super3v3.png
- **Logo meaning**: Custom family superhero logo representing the three kids
- **Additional elements**: Sometimes adds small capes or accessories for heroic effect

### Fantasy Sequence Additions
- **Sea Captain Scene**: Sailor outfit or nautical accessories (her suggestion!)
- **Punk Rock Scene**: Mini leather jacket over Super3 shirt, bass guitar
- **Various careers**: Always maintains the blue Super3 shirt as foundation
- **Hair accessories**: Sometimes ribbons or bands to keep hair controlled during activities
