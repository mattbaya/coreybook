# Character Profile: REMI
*The Brave Leader - Twin Brother*

## PHYSICAL CHARACTERISTICS

### Hair & Head
- **Dark curly/wavy hair** - medium length, thick and healthy
- **Energetic hair movement** - often flying or tousled from activity
- **Distinctive curls** that help identify him in crowd scenes

### Facial Features & Build
- **Age**: 11 years old
- **Twin to Oona** - similar height and build to his sister
- **Energetic, determined expression**
- **Often leading or taking charge** in group situations
- **Brave, confident demeanor**

## CLOTHING & ACCESSORIES

### Signature Outfit
- **BLUE T-SHIRT** with **SUPER3 LOGO** prominently displayed
- **Logo Details**: Red diamond shield with large yellow "3" in center, plus small letters "Z", "O", "R" in corners (for Zephyr, Oona, Remi) - see reference image: images/super3v3.png
- **Logo meaning**: Custom family superhero logo representing the three kids
- **Additional elements**: Sometimes adds small capes or accessories for extra heroic effect

### Fantasy Sequence Additions
- **Punk Rock Scene**: Tiny leather jacket over Super3 shirt, drumsticks in hand
- **Sea Captain Scene**: Cabin boy outfit or sailor accessories
- **Astronaut Scene**: Mini spacesuit or space helmet
- **Various careers**: Always maintains the blue Super3 shirt as his base

## PERSONALITY & CHARACTERISTICS

### Core Traits
- **Natural leader** - often leads the charge in family activities
- **Brave and energetic** - faces challenges head-on
- **Protective of siblings** - especially as older twin
- **Enthusiastic supporter** - believes completely in his dad's potential
- **Creative contributor** - offers wild career suggestions

### Family Dynamics
- **Twin relationship with Oona** - close partnership, similar energy
- **Big brother to Zephyr** - protective but loving
- **Dad's champion** - sees infinite potential in Corey
- **Part of the "Super Three"** - team identity is important

### Signature Career Suggestions
- **"You could be a PUNK ROCK GUITARIST!"** - his big fantasy contribution
- **"You could be a PROFESSIONAL MATTRESS TESTER!"** - practical but fun idea
- **Various other energetic suggestions** throughout the brainstorming
