# Character Profile: ZEPHYR
*The Littlest & Loudest - The Heart*

## PHYSICAL CHARACTERISTICS

### Hair & Head
- **Light brown hair** - medium length, slightly messy and tousled
- **Naturally unkempt** - not neat like her siblings, more free-spirited
- **Soft brown tones** that complement her energetic personality
- **Hair often moving** from her constant motion and enthusiasm

### Facial Features & Build
- **Age**: 9 years old (youngest of the three)
- **Smallest** in height and build compared to her older siblings
- **BIGGEST SMILE** of all the children - huge, radiant, infectious
- **Enormous, expressive eyes** that shine with hope and love
- **Most animated facial expressions** - everything shows on her face

## CLOTHING & ACCESSORIES

### Signature Outfit
- **BLUE T-SHIRT** with **SUPER3 LOGO** prominently displayed
- **Logo Details**: Red diamond shield with large yellow "3" in center, plus small letters "Z", "O", "R" in corners (for Zephyr, Oona, Remi) - see reference image: images/super3v3.png
- **Logo meaning**: Custom family superhero logo representing the three kids
- **Shirt might be slightly oversized** on her smaller frame

### Fantasy Sequence Additions
- **Astronaut scene**: Tiny spacesuit or space helmet (her big suggestion!)
- **Various careers**: Always in her blue Super3 shirt as foundation
- **Sometimes carries drawings** or creative contributions to family discussions

## PERSONALITY & CHARACTERISTICS

### Core Traits
- **LOUDEST** despite being smallest - compensates with maximum volume and energy
- **Most enthusiastic** - her excitement is infectious and boundless
- **Pure heart** - represents innocent love and absolute faith
- **Creative dreamer** - comes up with wild, imaginative ideas
- **Emotional center** - her feelings drive key story moments

### Family Dynamics
- **Baby sister** - youngest of the Super Three, but not treated as helpless
- **Daddy's girl** - special bond with Corey, sees him as capable of anything
- **Looks up to twins** - follows Remi and Oona's lead but adds her own energy
- **Heart of the family** - her emotions often reflect the family's feelings

### Signature Story Moments
- **"Papa, you can do ANYTHING!"** - her pivotal emotional speech to Corey
- **Astronaut suggestion** - her major career fantasy contribution (Space Café on Mars!)
- **Hand-holding scene** - taking Corey's hand in both of her small ones
- **The loudest shouting** during family rescue and celebrations
